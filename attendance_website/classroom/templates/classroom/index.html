About Our Website

What is Présence?

Présence is a French word for being present or to be in attendance.Our website works as an attendance management system which uses facial recognition.

How it works for Students?

Students can select a session created by the teacher and mark their attendance using facial recogniton.

How it works for Teachers?

Teachers can create sessions for students to mark their attendance and after that download the attendance in an excel sheet.

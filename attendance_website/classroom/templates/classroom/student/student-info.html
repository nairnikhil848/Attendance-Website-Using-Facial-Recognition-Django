Instructions for Students

MUST FOLLOW STEPS BEFORE MARKING ATTENDANCE

1.To be able to mark your attendance you need to upload your photo which will be captured in realtime.

2.While your photo is being captured make sure that the lighting conditions are good and stay still.

3.The captured photo will be used to recognize your face when your are giving your attendance in the future.



STEPS FOR MARKING ATTENDANCE

1.Select the session for which you want to mark your attendance.

2.Click on the mark attendance button.

3.Your attendance will be marked after recognizing your face.
